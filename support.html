Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first card? +

To create your first card, go to the Templates page, choose a template that suits your needs, and click "Edit Now". You'll be taken to the editor where you can customize your design.

How do I download my designs? +

Once you've finished editing your design, click the "Download" button in the editor. You can choose from various formats including PNG, JPG, and PDF.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
